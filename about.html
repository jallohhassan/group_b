About Our Club

G-COMPLEX is a private own enterprise that is located in the Northern part of the country. The club derived its name from a traditional beliefs of the pople in it community. According to the people there was a devil located at the back of it structure called "GBRUBEH"(G)living in a stream at the back of the Club. According to the people, the name of this devil means "WATER WILL NEVER DRAIN FROM THE STREAM" owing to this fact the people thought it fit to name the club after the devil's name. G-COMPLEX in which the G stands for the devil's name (GBRUBEH)
